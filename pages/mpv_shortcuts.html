mpv shortcuts

mpv is a free (as in freedom) media player.

The graphical interface is barebone, and I like it that way. You use the keyboard to interact with the media: there's no need to search through menus with your mouse, just press the correct button on your keyboard. My problem is that I can never remember the shortcuts.

You can always consult the manual, either online or locally with man mpv.

I prefer to have a limited list of the ones I actually use:

p / SPACE  - Pause/Unpause
LEFT/RIGHT - Seek backward/forward 5 sec
UP/DOWN    - Seek forward/backward 1 min
ENTER      - Go forward in the playlist
q          - Stop playing and quit

[ and ]    - Decrease/increase current playback speed by 10%
BACKSPACE  - Reset playback speed to normal

#          - Cycle through the available audio tracks
Ctrl +/-   - Adjust audio delay by +/- 0.1 seconds
m          - Mute sound

v          - Toggle subtitle visibility
j and J    - Cycle through the available subtitles
x and z    - Adjust subtitle delay by +/- 0.1 seconds
r and t    - Move subtitles up/down

s          - Take a screenshot
f          - Toggle fullscreen

1 and 2    - Adjust contrast.
3 and 4    - Adjust brightness.
5 and 6    - Adjust gamma.
7 and 8    - Adjust saturation

Put that somewhere handy, like the home directory.
